Data

Here are the datasets provided in the book for download.

  • temp-diffs.csv — This is the difference in temperatures for North American weather stations over the periods of their observations. This is used in chapter 2, on GIS analysis.

  • ufo.json — This is the cleaned-up UFO dataset from Infochimps. This is used in chapter 4, on classifying UFO descriptions.

  • post-sample-100000.json.gz — This is the random sample of data from the StackOverflow data dump. This is used in chapter 9, on social network data.
